Welcome to Page 3... And a tribute to Akira Toriyama



R.I.P. & Thank You, Akira Toriyama


April 5, 1955 - March 1, 2024



What's the meaning behind this?


REASON #1
Akira Toriyama created Dragonball, the anime that paved way, and responsible for where anime is at present day.

REASON #2
Akira Toriyama essentially is the godfather of anime. He inspired the authors of One Piece, Naruto, and Bleach.
Then, the newer generations of anime are also inspired from these 3 anime, and also from Dragonball.

REASON #3
Many YouTubers who make Dragonball content are also inspired and had their lives changed because of Dragonball, whether animation, workout, life, and many more.

Big examples are:
Hyourinjutsu - One of, if not, the best in DB Pixel animation
SeeReax, Dotodoya, and Rhymestyle - Some of the leading DB YouTubers, together known as "The Three Idiots"
KSI - considered one of the biggest YouTubers who is known to be a huge fan of Dragonball,
and to be an inspiration for how far he's come now.

GENERAL REASON
Akira Toriyama inspired millions of people in the world, especially in the animation industry, and almost the entirety of anime & its community.
He was essentially a father figure, and a role model to many, thanks to what he had done.
It is why such a tribute exists here. A moment of silence and appreciation to Akira Toriyama, who has now gone further beyond.
